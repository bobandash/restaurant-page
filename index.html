Company Mission

In Japan, omurice is a staple food, found anywhere from the local mom and pop store to high rise luxury Michellin star restaurants. In America, omurice treated as something easy and fast -- something that anyone can make, given the proper ingredients at home. As a Los Angeles native, the owner GoldBeaver witnessed his favorite omurice restaurants go bankrupt because of this preconception. At Omulife, we are trying to change the perception of omurice in America. We offer irreplicable omurice experience.
